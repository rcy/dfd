DFD New Year's Day

Have your resolution for this year to be (a) present to yourself, to your joys and woes, to your sufferings and jubilations, loves and hates, ups and downs. Notice how it all flows. Changing, changing.

We need not anesthetize ourselves with constant distractions of shopping, consuming and discarding people and things to get us through the days, marathon watchings of videos, endless 'searching' on-line for anything to numb us from our discomfort of being alone. It is okay.

Break through all of these, break free and truly enjoy life.

Experience yourself.

Please join and embrace the world revolution from within. Whether you are concerned about global climate change, or human rights and any good cause, all of them will be helped by having more and more people with calm and compassionate minds.

When you allow yourself to experience sides of yourself, your shadow side, your anger, hatred...and not act on them, you will grow in self-acceptance, self love and from that will be borne compassion for all beings who are suffering. You will see people suffering, their addictions through the lens of 'yes that is in me too, I have experienced that, I have compassion for you and me, too!'

Invite your friends and lets change the world one person, one moment at a time.

DFD Giftless Holiday Season

Every year our society goes wild over consumerism because that is how we are trained, inculcated from childhood. This needs to come to a close.

The rampant consumerism has NOTHING to do with any of the traditional celebrations that occur at the end of the Western calendar year.

So put a stop to all the destruction required for this consumerism, the stress, the addiction, the craving, the harm and let the Earth Breathe.

Give the Earth something back for a change.

Give Her the gift of a Giftless Holiday Season.
